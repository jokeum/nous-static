「危險發生需要多少時間？10秒、一分鐘？」
Ho long does dangers occur in mid-night ?
10 minutes ? Or just within 1 minute.

根據統計：警察接獲報案，到達犯罪現場要10分鐘。
在警察到達之前，除非身有百般武藝，危險關頭誰能救命？
According to statistics, officers take above 10 minutes arriving at crime scene.
Before police coming, nobody can’t escape from dangers unless they have defense skills against criminal in such long time.

【夜歸守護者】以智慧路燈的概念出發，
Night Guardian is an idea with “ smart street lights.”
在危害人身安全的犯罪情況發生時，
街頭攝影機會連線雲端進行分析，以「圖像辨識技術」偵測並辨別犯罪動作。
確認是犯罪動作後，串聯周遭500公尺內的智慧路燈，啟動立體音效，模擬環繞警車鳴笛聲。
Street Camera with cloud computing will detect attack behaviors by ‘Pattern Recognition’ while people are going to suffer from dangers.
Next, cloud connecting will connect street lights network in 500 m, and switching on stereo speakers installed on street lights.
Finally, all street lights will simulate stereo police siren to stop crime.

第一時間嚇阻犯人，在警察抵達前，搶先保命的黃金時機！
Smart street lights, protecting your life before police coming.
